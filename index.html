The Loft at 600 F is a vibrant, exciting, and inviting event space in the Penn Quarter/Chinatown neighborhood of Washington DC.

Perched on the third floor of a historic building across from the Verizon Center, the light-filled, modern space can be set in various layouts ranging from a comfy lounge to a formal conference table or from a engaging lecture to an inviting happy hour.

Chic furnishings and exposed brick are accented with modern technology in the perfect downtown location making The Loft at 600 F the best new place to meet in DC.

The Space

 
 

Total Square Footage: 2,152 square feet

Furniture/Equipment*

  • Custom sofas, available in various setups
  • Movable bars
  • Coffee table, side tables, and credenzas
  • Up-lighting and accent lighting
  • Highboy round tables
  • 6' x 15' Classroom tables and black rectangular linens
  • Standing podium
  • Easels
  • Coat racks
  • Folding chairs
  • Microwave, kitchen sink and mini-fridge

A/V Capabilities

  • HD projector
  • HDMI
  • 10' widescreen projection screen
  • Surround sound receiver
  • BluRay player
  • Mac Mini
  • Apple TV
  • 55" HD television
  • WiFi

The Loft at 600 F is a versatile space, available for whatever your event needs may be. The light-filled, modern space can be set in various layouts ranging from a comfy lounge to a formal conference table or from a engaging lecture to an inviting happy hour.

Perfect for happy hours, networking events, book signings, meet-and-greets, as a pre- or after party lounge and so much more. Exposed brick, large windows and cozy modern touches distinguish this space as the hip new place to meet.

* Furniture and equipment listed is complimentary with all Loft reservations.
